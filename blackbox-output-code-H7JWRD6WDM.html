About Us

Nestled in Al Rimal District, Riyadh, 4F for Friends is your go-to spot for quality coffee and unforgettable moments with loved ones. Established around 2019, we offer a trendy yet classic atmosphere with comfortable seating, Wi-Fi, and a focus on halal, vegetarian-friendly options. Rated 4.5+ stars on Google, we're praised for our friendly staff and clean environment.

Open daily 7 AM–12 AM. Closed during Ramadan fasting hours.
